ABOUT ME

Hey! I'm Anna, also known as Kudori. Since 2022, I've been working as a Graphic Designer, creating visual experiences that help brands and streamers stand out. I specialize in Twitch graphics - including overlays, emotes, announcement visuals, and banners - as well as web and social media design, such as logos, mascots, style guides, and YouTube thumbnails.
